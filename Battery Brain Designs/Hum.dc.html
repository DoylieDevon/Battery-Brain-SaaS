How it works

Set it once. Save every day.

STEP 1

Connect in 2 minutes

Sign in to your GivEnergy and Octopus accounts. No installer, no wiring — Hum talks to them for you.

STEP 2

Hum learns your home

It studies your usage and tomorrow's Octopus prices, then plans the perfect charge — full before peak, every time.

STEP 3

Relax — you're covered

Run on your own stored power through the pricey evening. Check in anytime to see what you saved.

The payoff

Never pay peak prices again.

A fully-cycled battery on the Octopus Cosy tariff is worth up to £2.80 a day. Hum makes sure none of that value is left on the table.
